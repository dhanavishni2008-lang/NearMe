Vellore is home to several notable Sai Baba temples, including the unique Sri Nava Sai Baba Temple near Pallikonda, which features nine distinct Sai Baba idols representing the Navagrahas (planets), and the Arulmigu SeshaSai Sri Sai Baba Temple in Abdullahpuram, which also includes a Siddha Hospital and a peaceful atmosphere
